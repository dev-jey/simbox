Community Guidelines

  2. Community Guidelines

1. Respect everyone.

No matter their race, ethnicity, national origin, sex, gender, religious affiliation, or disabilities.


2. Swearing & Explicit language is prohibited.

Using this language in SimBox.org is not welcomed no matter how it is shaped or formed.


3. Any NSFW content or comments.

Posting any NSFW content or comments or anything related to it in SimBox.org is prohibited.


4. Harassment Bullying or Hate Speech.

We do not welcome any harassment against SimBox’s Staff or users.


5. Do Not

Defame, libel, ridicule, mock, stalk, threaten, harass, intimidate or Abuse anyone of SimBox’s users.


5. Do Not

Engage in conduct that is fraudulent, illegal or otherwise harmful to SimBox or its users.


5. Do Not

Attempt to obtain passwords or other private information from other members.


5. Do Not

Improperly use support channels or complaint buttons to make false reports to us.


5. Do Not

Upload or transmit (or attempt to upload or transmit) files that contain viruses, Trojan horses, worms, time bombs, cancelbots, corrupted files or data, or any other similar software or programs or engage in any other activity that may damage the operation of the Service or other users' computers.


5. Do Not

Violate the contractual, personal, intellectual property or other rights of any party including using, uploading, transmitting, distributing, or otherwise making available any information made available through the Service in any manner that infringes any copyright, trademark, patent, trade secret, or other right of any party.
